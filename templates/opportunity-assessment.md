### Business Viability

## Overall Assessment

**Total Score:** ___/25 (5 dimensions × 5 points each)

**Scoring Guide:**
- 20-25: Strong opportunity - Recommend GO for full discovery
- 15-19: Promising opportunity - Recommend EXPLORE with more research
- 10-14: Uncertain opportunity - Recommend HOLD or deeper analysis
- Below 10: Weak opportunity - Recommend NO GO

**Recommendation:**
- [ ] **GO** - Pursue full discovery (proceed to problem validation)
- [ ] **EXPLORE** - Gather more information before deciding
- [ ] **HOLD** - Revisit later when conditions change
- [ ] **NO GO** - Not a priority, archive for future consideration
